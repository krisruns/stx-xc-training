## **PACE GUIDE (ALL GROUPS)**

- **@E:** Easy pace (conversational, 65–75% max HR)
- **@T:** Threshold pace (comfortably hard, 15K–HM race effort, 85–90% max HR)
- **@I:** Interval pace (VO2 max, 3K–5K race effort, 95–100% max HR)
- **@R:** Repetition pace (speed work, mile pace or faster, neuromuscular power)
- **@RP:** Race pace (current 5K fitness level)

**Fartlek intervals:** "on" = controlled surge at roughly 5K–10K effort; "steady" = comfortable recovery pace (not walking)  
**Progression runs:** All groups run the same time structure at their own appropriate effort level — pacing by feel, not by watch
