Pace Guide

@E — Easy
Conversational, 65–75% max HR. Default recovery and base-building pace.
@T — Threshold
Comfortably hard, 15K–HM race effort, 85–90% max HR.
@I — Interval
VO₂ max effort, 3K–5K race effort, 95–100% max HR.
@R — Repetition
Speed work, mile pace or faster. Neuromuscular power development.
@RP — Race Pace
Current 5K fitness level. Race-specific rhythm development.
Fartlek / Progression
Running by feel. All groups use same time structure; pace self-determined by effort.
