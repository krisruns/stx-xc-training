TRAINING NOTES
  • First Progression Runs: Introduction to building pace during the workout - steady to faster but controlled
  • Surge Progression: Week 3 Update - Now 5 surges x 20 seconds @ 5k effort
  • Progression Guidelines: Start steady pace, gradually increase. "Faster but controlled" - NOT race effort
  • Surge Location: Perform surges DURING the run (not after). Easy runs = last 15 minutes; Long runs = last 20 minutes
  • Recovery: Maintain normal running pace between surges (do not slow to jog)
  • Easy Run Guidelines: Conversational pace throughout, should be able to speak in full sentences
  • Post-Workout: Complete mobility and strength work immediately after run while muscles are warm
  • Strength Training Added: Week 3 introduces structured strength work to complement mobility
  • Freshman Preparation: Next week (Week 4) freshman training begins!
